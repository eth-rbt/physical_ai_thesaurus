# Candidate Project Atoms — Interaction-Design Research

A vetted shortlist to rebalance the thesaurus away from its commercial/industrial skew, toward
HCI / interaction-design **research** (the Ishii / Weiser lineage: tangible bits, ubicomp, calm &
ambient computing, shape-changing matter, on-skin interfaces, speculative design, IoT objects).

**Status:** review shortlist — nothing authored yet. ~50 distinct new candidates below, grouped by
thread, plus an 11-atom backbone already verified. Every citation was fetched against a primary
source and adversarially re-verified (52/52 survived); corrected titles/authors are reflected here.

**Method:** two passes of the deep-research harness + a 13-thread top-up workflow (one research +
one adversarial verifier agent per thread). `confidence` and any `correction` are carried through.

**Excluded as already-present atoms:** Weiser ubicomp vision · Xerox PARC tabs/pads/boards ·
Project Soli · Little Signals (ATAP) · Kismet · Nabaztag.

Suggested `project_type` ∈ {`academic_paper`, `research_prototype`, `concept`}. Categories ∈
{Embodiment, Intelligence, Function, Genealogy}.

## 1 · Shape-changing & actuated displays (beyond inFORM)

- **Relief: A Scalable Actuated Shape Display** — Leithinger & Ishii · TEI 2010 · https://www.media.mit.edu/publications/relief-a-scalable-actuated-shape-display/ (DOI 10.1145/1709886.1709928)
  120-pin actuated tabletop that renders 2.5D terrain and senses push/pull. *The direct ancestor of inFORM/TRANSFORM.* → `academic_paper` · Embodiment, Genealogy · enriches **Form**, shape-changing genealogy. **high**
- **Lumen: Interactive Visual and Shape Display for Calm Computing** — Poupyrev, Nashida, Maruyama, Rekimoto, Yamaji · SIGGRAPH 2004 E-Tech · https://history.siggraph.org/experience/lumen-interactive-visual-and-shape-display-for-calm-computing-by-poupyrev-nashida-maruyama-rekimoto-and-yamaji/ (DOI 10.1145/1186155.1186173)
  13×13 "RGBH" SMA display — each pixel shows color *and* rises physically. Bridges shape-change ↔ calm/ambient. → `academic_paper` · Embodiment, Function, Genealogy · **Form**, Ambient, **Modalities of Expression**. **high**
- **ShapeClip: Towards Rapid Prototyping with Shape-Changing Displays for Designers** — Hardy, Weichel, Taher, Vidler, Alexander · CHI 2015 · https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/2702123.2702599
  Modular z-actuating clips driven by screen luminance — democratizes the inFORM paradigm. → `academic_paper` · Embodiment, Function · **Form**, Modalities of Expression. **high**
- **Grand Challenges in Shape-Changing Interface Research** — Alexander, Roudaut, Steimle, Hornbæk, Bruns Alonso, Follmer, Merritt · CHI 2018 · https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3173574.3173873
  Field-defining agenda paper — ideal connective tissue / overview atom. → `concept` · Genealogy, Embodiment, Function · anchors a **Shape-changing interfaces** card. **high**

> *Merged:* **TRANSFORM** (Lexus Milan 2014 exhibition / "TRANSFORM as Adaptive and Dynamic Furniture", CHI 2015 EA, DOI 10.1145/2702613.2732494) — see §11; one atom.

## 2 · Graspable & tabletop tangibles

- **Urp: A Luminous-Tangible Workbench for Urban Planning and Design** — Underkoffler & Ishii · CHI 1999 · https://dblp.org/rec/conf/chi/UnderkofflerI99.html

- **Sensetable: A Wireless Object Tracking Platform for TUIs** — Patten, Ishii, Hines, Pangaro · CHI 2001 · https://dblp.org/rec/conf/chi/PattenIHP01.html

- **Illuminating Clay: A 3-D Tangible Interface for Landscape Analysis** — Piper, Ratti, Ishii · CHI 2002 · https://dblp.org/rec/conf/chi/PiperRI02.html

- **The reacTable** — Jordà, Geiger, Alonso, Kaltenbrunner · TEI 2007 · https://dblp.org/rec/conf/tei/JordaGAK07.html

## 3 · Ambient / calm media (peripheral display)

- **Dangling String (Live Wire)** — Natalie Jeremijenko · Xerox PARC artist-in-residence · 1995 · https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natalie_Jeremijenko
  8-ft motorized string twitching with Ethernet traffic — Weiser's canonical calm-tech exemplar. *Artwork/installation, not a paper.* → `research_prototype` · Genealogy, Function, Embodiment · Calm/Ambient genealogy, **Modalities of Expression**. **high**
- **Water Lamp and Pinwheels: Ambient Projection of Digital Information into Architectural Space** — Dahley, Wisneski, Ishii · CHI '98 Conf. Summary · https://tangible.media.mit.edu/project/pinwheels/
  "Ambient fixtures": water-ripple shadows + pinwheels in a "bit wind." Sibling to ambientROOM. → `research_prototype` · Function, Embodiment, Genealogy · Ambient, Tangible Bits genealogy. **high**
- **Pinwheels: Visualizing Information Flow in an Architectural Space** — Ishii, Ren, Frei · CHI 2001 EA · https://tangible.media.mit.edu/project/pinwheels/ (DOI 10.1145/634067.634135)
  Room-scale array of ~40 pinwheels spinning to data streams. *Can fold into the entry above as one "Pinwheels/ambient fixtures" atom.* → `research_prototype` · Function, Embodiment · Ambient, **Spatial Relationship**. **high**
- **Ambient Orb (Ambient Devices)** — Rose, Resner, Hyatt, Gandhi · MIT Media Lab spin-off · 2002 · https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ambient_device
  Frosted orb mapping one live data stream to a glanceable color glow. *Bridges calm-tech research → consumer IoT.* → `research_prototype` · Function, Embodiment, Genealogy · Ambient, ambient-intelligence genealogy. **high** *(also listed under §12; one atom)*

## 4 · Product-as-interface (everyday objects as UI)

- **Marble Answering Machine** — Durrell Bishop · RCA · 1992 (documented I.D. Magazine 1995) · https://dataphys.org/list/durrell-bishops-marble-answering-machine/
  A marble ejected per voice message; place it in a dimple to play. Canonical proto-TUI. *Concept; article-author attribution debated (Poynor vs. Crampton Smith).* → `concept` · Embodiment, Function, Genealogy · proto-Tangible-Bits genealogy, **Form**. **medium**
- **MediaCup: experience with design and use of computer-augmented everyday artefacts** — Beigl, Gellersen, Schmidt · Computer Networks 35(4) · 2001 · https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1389128600001808
  An ordinary coffee cup augmented to become context-aware & networked. → `research_prototype` · Intelligence, Embodiment, Genealogy · **Context Awareness**, ubicomp genealogy. **high**
- **Smart-Its: computers for artifacts in the physical world** — Gellersen, Kubach, Beigl, Schmidt, Holmquist, et al. · CACM 48(3) · 2005 (platform from Ubicomp 2001) · https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/1047671.1047707
  Tiny modules that make any artifact sense/compute/connect; "Smart-Its Friends" pair objects by shaking. → `research_prototype` · Intelligence, Embodiment, Genealogy · ubicomp genealogy, **Context Awareness**. **high**
- **Pin&Play: Networking Objects through Pins** — Van Laerhoven, Schmidt, Gellersen · UbiComp 2002 (LNCS 2498) · https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/3-540-45809-3_17
  Conductive surfaces where pinned objects get power + network. Placement as configuration. → `research_prototype` · Embodiment, Function, Genealogy · **Spatial Relationship**, ubicomp genealogy. **high**
- **Things That Think (TTT) Consortium** — Gershenfeld, Hawley, Machover, Paradiso · MIT Media Lab · 1995 (book *When Things Start to Think*, 1999) · https://ttt.media.mit.edu/
  The umbrella vision of computation migrating into everyday objects. Sibling to Weiser. → `concept` · Genealogy, Intelligence, Embodiment · ubicomp genealogy. **high**

- **Lowest-confidence item:** Marble Answering Machine (medium) — the 1992 artifact is solid; the I.D. Magazine article author is debated (Poynor vs. Crampton Smith).

- **Image rights:** many are research figures / lab-page photos → `rights_status: needs_permission` or `academic_fair_use_candidate`; the two books and product objects need their own sourcing.
