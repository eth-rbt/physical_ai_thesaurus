# Card-First Project Discovery — Results

Top-down inversion of the thesaurus build: each **card** was treated as a question with a shape (an axis, a genealogy with time-depth, or a diverse grid), and a history-led web search hunted for the projects that best fill that shape — deliberately reaching **backward in time** for the foundational/origin instance, not just today's products.

**Method:** `card-first-project-discovery` workflow — 21 cards × (1 research + 1 adversarial verifier) = 42 Sonnet agents, ~1,140 web lookups. Every project was date- and existence-checked; verifier-rejected items are dropped. `✅ have` = already a project record in `src/content/projects/`; `🆕 missing` = not yet authored.

**Totals:** 276 verified projects across 21 cards; **162 are missing** (new research candidates). Covers the 11 Genealogy cards + the 14 built priority cards (4 genealogy cards overlap).

> Reading the table: **Slot** = position on the card's spectrum (origin→state-of-art). **Anchors** = the card this project most belongs to (may differ from the section it surfaced under). Confidence/corrections come from the verifier.
