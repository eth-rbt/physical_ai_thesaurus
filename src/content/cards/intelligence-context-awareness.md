Context awareness is how much of its situation a system can actually read — and the range here is wide. At one end, glasses and phone cameras (Ray-Ban Meta, Google Lens) answer questions about whatever you point them at; at the other, ambient sensors quietly model a whole space — Soli's radar feeling presence in the dark, Nest learning your schedule, Home Assistant fusing room-by-room state, and Cordyceps' objects pooling what each one sees into one coordinated picture.
