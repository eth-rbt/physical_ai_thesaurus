The home assistant grew up in stages: from faceless voice speakers like the Echo and HomePod, to countertop screens like the Nest Hub and Echo Show that show answers as well as speak them. Behind the hardware sit the connectors — assistant layers like Google Assistant, platforms like SmartThings and Home Assistant, and the open Matter standard — that tie devices from rival brands into one coordinated home.
