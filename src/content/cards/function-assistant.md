Assistants are the AI helpers you summon to answer, fetch, and act, and they live in strikingly different bodies — voice-only speakers, the phones and watches you already carry, glasses on your face, and dedicated pocket gadgets. As the interaction model shifts from typed or spoken commands toward multimodal AI that also sees through a camera, the assistant increasingly responds to whatever is in front of you rather than only to what you say.
