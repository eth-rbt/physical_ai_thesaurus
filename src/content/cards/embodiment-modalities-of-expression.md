These systems range from a smart bulb that expresses only through color and brightness — an appliance you never mistake for a being — to faces and bodies like Kismet and Moxie that move eyes, ears, and lips into readable emotion and clearly read as social actors. In between they spread across channels: light (Hue, Little Signals), sound and ambient motion (Nabaztag, Paro), doglike posture (Aibo), animated faces (Jibo, Vector), and voice with attitude (Stochastic Parrot).
