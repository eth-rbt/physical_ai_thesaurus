Every device here turns part of your life into a record, and the range is in what it captures and where that record ends up: always-on home cameras and microphones (Ring, Nest Cam, Echo) and body-worn recorders (Ray-Ban Meta, Humane, Limitless) stream video, audio, and even the people around you up to a company's cloud, while the Apple Watch keeps much of its biometric sensing on-device and Home Assistant is built local-first so the data stays on hardware you own.
